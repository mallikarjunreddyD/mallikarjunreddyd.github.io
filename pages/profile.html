Brief Profile

Dr. Dorsala Mallikarjun Reddy is an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Information Technology Sri City, India. He received his Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology Warangal in collaboration with the Institute for Development and Research in Banking Technology, Hyderabad, and holds an M.Tech. degree in Computer Science from Andhra University.

Prior to his academic tenure, he worked as a Blockchain Researcher and Lead at McKinley Rice, now known as Red Rob, where he contributed to a novel blockchain architecture tailored for the advertisement industry for the client iCraft, Korea.

His work has received recognition through participation and awards in blockchain-focused hackathons and buildathons, and his research interests include blockchain networks, distributed systems, verifiable computation, and federated systems.
